# Execution Philosophy

## Product Thesis

Win local-service lead generation by owning the full loop:

1. Capture high-intent demand from paid and owned channels.
2. Score fast so weak leads do not waste buyer trust.
3. Route only to buyers with real capacity and geographic fit.
4. Measure close-rate feedback so pricing and campaign budgets get smarter each week.

## What to optimize first

- Speed to contact: the first five minutes matter more than extra dashboard complexity.
- Buyer trust: lead quality and routing discipline are the core product.
- Source visibility: every lead must keep source, campaign, city, and service metadata.
- Repeatability: each city and service should be launchable from the same funnel and playbook pattern.

## Expansion model

- Phase 1: run city and service landing pages, paid traffic, and partner funnels into the intake API.
- Phase 2: connect approved ad platform webhooks, telephony, and email delivery.
- Phase 3: add conversion feedback from buyers so bids and pricing become dynamic.
- Phase 4: split buyer inventory by exclusivity tier, SLA, and close rate.

## What not to build around

- Unauthorized scraping from Facebook or other platforms
- Massive cold outreach without consent
- Buyer assignment rules that ignore geography or quality floor
- Dashboards with no operational hooks into intake, routing, and follow-up
